Why this tool is useful

This platform helps healthcare stakeholders turn historical claims and disease data into practical decisions. It combines business KPIs, payer analytics, disease burden, and forecasting into a single workflow so users can quickly understand what has happened, what is happening now, and what is likely to happen next.

For Insurance Teams

Insurance users can monitor total claims, claim cost trends, payer leakage, and coverage performance. This helps identify where money is being lost, which claim categories are driving cost, and where payer strategy or reimbursement controls may need improvement.

  • Track actual vs forecast claim cost
  • Identify top leakage payers
  • Review claim type distribution
  • Support cost planning and pricing decisions

For Pharma Teams

Pharma users can evaluate disease burden, view opportunity signals, and analyze SARIMA-based demand forecasts for selected diseases. This supports therapeutic prioritization, market access planning, and forward-looking portfolio decisions.

  • Compare disease demand patterns
  • Use forecast-driven tactical filtering
  • Evaluate opportunity matrix signals
  • Prioritize high-potential disease areas

How end users can make decisions with this tool

1. Understand the current situation

Start by reviewing KPIs, claims trends, and disease burden to understand where cost and demand are concentrated.

2. Identify risk or opportunity

Use leakage charts, payer analysis, and opportunity matrix views to isolate segments that deserve attention.

3. Plan the next action

Use forecast outputs to plan interventions, allocate resources, improve contracts, or prioritize disease strategy.
