Forecast Methodology

Future claim costs were estimated using a Linear Regression model developed in Python.

The model was trained on historical monthly healthcare data to capture underlying trends and patterns.

Key predictors include:
TimeIndex – long-term trend
TotalPatients – healthcare demand
TotalEncounters – utilization levels

Forecasts were generated for the next six months to support forward-looking cost planning.
